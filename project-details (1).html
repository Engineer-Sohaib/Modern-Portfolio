Overview

The Challenge

Teams were struggling with scattered tools, communication gaps, and lack of visibility into project progress. Existing solutions were either too complex, expensive, or lacked essential collaboration features.

The Solution

We built TaskFlow — an all-in-one platform that combines project management, team collaboration, and analytics in a simple, intuitive interface that teams love to use.

My Approach

I led the entire development process from research and planning to design, development, and deployment. Focused on building a scalable, performant, and beautiful product with great UX.
